What This Is About

A cancer diagnosis changes everything.

Not because it breaks you — but because it strips away what was never really yours to begin with. It cuts through the noise. The performance. The "should."

And in that space, something essential becomes visible.

Your purpose. Your meaning. The life you are actually here to live.

This waitinglist is for those who have been through cancer — or are close to someone who has — and feel the pull toward something more intentional. More true. More aligned with who they discovered themselves to be.

Your Guide

About Leo

Leo Boxem — guide, mentor, and cancer survivor.

Twelve years ago, Leo was diagnosed with leukemia.

In that moment, something became undeniably clear: she had not yet lived the life she was here to live.

That realization did not come from fear. It came from truth.

It became the foundation of everything she does today — guiding women through the most honest moments of their lives, helping them reconnect with their purpose, and showing them that the path forward is not despite what happened. It is because of it.

Leo knows this journey from the inside.

+ David — Co-Facilitator, Thriving After Cancer Retreats

David brings his own expertise and lived experience to the retreats. Together, Leo and David hold a space where survivors can find their footing — not just physically, but in purpose and direction.
