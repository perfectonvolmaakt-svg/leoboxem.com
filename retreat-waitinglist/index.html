What to Expect

Leo's retreats take place on Ibiza — an island that holds a particular energy for women who are ready to shift something.

Each retreat is intimate, intentional, and created around one clear purpose: to help you remember who you are, and what you are here for.

The group is always small. The work is always deep. And the island does the rest.

Three different retreats. Each with its own focus. All sharing one thread — the truth that there is more for you. And that you know it.

About Leo

Leo Boxem — guide, mentor, and retreat host on Ibiza.

Leo has hosted retreats on Ibiza for years. Each one is designed from the inside out — not as a getaway, but as a going-in.

She chooses Ibiza deliberately. The island has a quality of stripping things back — of making the important things feel clear and the unimportant things fall away.

Women arrive full. Full of other people's expectations, unfinished decisions, the noise of daily life.

They leave lighter. Clearer. More themselves.

Spaces are always limited. The waitinglist is where it begins.
