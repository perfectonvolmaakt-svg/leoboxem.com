A global movement for women in transition.

Remember Your Future™ is not a program. It is a way of living — and a community of women who have decided to stop playing small and start living truthfully.

Leo Boxem founded this movement after her own turning point — a leukemia diagnosis that made clear she had not yet lived the life she was here to live.

Today, thousands of women around the world have been part of this community. They are in transition. They are in the process of becoming. And they are doing it — together, for themselves.
