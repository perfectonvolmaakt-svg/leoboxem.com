What Is a Heart-Journal™?

A Heart-Journal is not a diary. It is not a sketchbook. It is not art therapy.

It is a practice — a way of meeting yourself on paper, without judgment, without rules, without needing to be "good at it."

Women who do this work are often surprised by what comes out. Not just drawings or words — but clarity. Relief. A sense of recognition.

It is one of the most powerful, accessible tools Leo has encountered in over a decade of guiding women through transition.

The challenge runs for a defined period, with guidance from Leo. Thousands of women have done it before. Many say it changed the way they see themselves.

About Leo

Leo Boxem — guide, mentor, and creator of the Heart-Journal™ Challenge.

Leo has been guiding women through transition for over a decade. The Heart-Journal™ Challenge emerged from her own creative practice — a tool she discovered could unlock something in women that years of talking sometimes could not reach.

More than 4,500 women have joined previous editions. Many describe it as one of the most unexpectedly powerful things they have done.

Leo brings the same warmth, honesty, and depth to this creative challenge that she brings to all of her work.

This is not about being artistic. It is about being honest with yourself, and igniting your creative spark (that is already inside you).
