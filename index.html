About Leo

Leo Boxem is a guide for women in transition.

Her work is grounded in lived experience, deep listening, and practical wisdom.

She does not believe in quick fixes. She believes in honest change.

She doesn't believe in years and years of healing old wounds. She has her own way of helping you rapidly heal in a way that actually lasts.

She doesn't chit-chat on the surface. She speaks about what truly matters — in a light way.

After facing life-altering challenges herself, Leo discovered that transformation does not begin with action. It begins with truth.

Today, she helps women recognize the transition they are in, reconnect with their purpose, fully express their life's work, and create meaningful change — one Authentic Shift™ at a time.

If you are reading this, you may be in that moment now.

You have built a life that works. But something inside you is asking for more honesty, more meaning, more alignment.

You are not looking for motivation. You are looking for direction — and the right environment to grow into your next chapter.
